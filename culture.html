RPG

RPG stands for "Role-Playing Game".

The RPG first debuted in the United States in 1971 with the establishment of The Fantasy Game (which is still played) and has since swept the world, now made more accessible to players thanks to the use of the Internet.

It is made up of a group of people who get together to tell a narrative. Throughout the game, the characters (players) go on various adventures that would be worthy of a film or stage production.

Medieval fantasy, horror trailers, space travel, and even historical settings are all examples of RPGs.
