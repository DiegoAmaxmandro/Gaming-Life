Sharing Experiences.

In a way video games were and are part of our lives, below we can see some stories about it and how people can be affected by something that comes well beyond just entertainment.


Paula Wendt, 26, Brazilian.

My first video game was a Nintendo 64, my grandfather gave it to me when I was only 8 years old, today I can guarantee that this gift made my childhood much happier, I still carry with me the love for Mario and all that Nintendo universe.

Leticia queiroz, 25, Brazilian

My life with video games started with the Sega Master System, my parents gave it to me when I was very young, the first game couldn't have been another one but sonic, I couldn't stop playing, time passed technology advanced but I still love the old games and consoles in their 2D simplicity.

Marina Dutra, 26, Brazilian.

When I was a child I only played portable video games until one day I came across the Playstation one, that console was something from another world that brought me many moments of happiness and continues to bring me to this day, always surprising me with new games. And I'm still in love with it all. but obviously I never abandoned my first one that remains on my shelf.
